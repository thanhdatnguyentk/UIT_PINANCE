Welcome to the help page. Below is a detailed guide on main usage steps and answers to frequently asked questions.

Detailed Feature Guide

  1. Registration & Login:
    • Access /register to create an account.
    • Fill in complete information and click "Register".
    • Login at /login with email and password.
  2. Dashboard:
    • View account overview, balance and transactions.
  3. Markets:
    • View prices, volume and historical price charts.
  4. Orders:
    • Choose BUY/SELL, enter price, volume, TP/SL (if needed), click "Place Order".
  5. Watchlist:
    • Automatically displays stocks you have purchased.
  6. Transaction History:
    • View completed transactions.
  7. Deposit/Withdraw:
    • Select account, enter amount and confirm.
  8. Personal Profile:
    • Cập nhật thông tin cá nhân và mật khẩu.
